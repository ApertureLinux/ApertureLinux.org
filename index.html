Description:

Aperture Linux Is designed to be a daily-driver Arch based Linux distribution, focusing on making the Arch experience smoother, and making the users life easier.

Features:

  • Multiple prebuilt AUR packages.
  • Rolling Release, no long waits for updates.
  • Minamalistic and lightweight, making sure to not bloat the system.
  • Neutralizes Artifical Intelligence Neurotoxin (We'll keep working on this one..)
